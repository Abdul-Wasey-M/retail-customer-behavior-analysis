# Business Problem Statement

## Background

A leading retail company wants to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve sales performance, customer satisfaction, and long-term customer loyalty. The company has observed changes in purchasing patterns across customer demographics, product categories, and purchasing preferences.

Management wants to identify the factors that influence customer purchasing decisions, including discounts, customer reviews, seasonal trends, subscription status, payment methods, and purchase frequency.

## Business Question

**How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?**

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Understand seasonal purchasing trends.
* Evaluate the impact of discounts and promotional activities.
* Analyze customer loyalty and repeat purchase behavior.
* Explore customer demographics and purchasing patterns.
* Generate actionable business insights through data analysis.

## Project Deliverables

### 1. Data Preparation & Modeling (Python)

* Clean and transform the raw dataset.
* Prepare data for analysis.

### 2. Data Analysis (SQL)

* Organize and analyze data using SQL queries.
* Extract insights related to customer behavior and purchasing trends.

### 3. Interactive Dashboard (Power BI)

* Build an interactive dashboard.
* Visualize key metrics and business insights.

### 4. Project Report & Presentation

* Summarize findings and recommendations.
* Present insights in a professional format.

### 5. GitHub Repository

* Maintain a well-structured repository containing project files, code, queries, dashboards, and documentation.
